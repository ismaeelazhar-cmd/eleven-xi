Build your XI

Spin the wheel to get squads from football history — World Cup, Euros, Champions League, and more. Pick 11 players for your chosen formation. Use rerolls to swap a spin you don't want.

👑

Pick a manager

Choose a tactical style (Attack +4 ATK, Defence +4 DEF, Gegenpress +2/+2) or spin the wheel for a famous historical manager. Their bonus chips show the exact effect on your team stats.

🏆

Simulate & score

Run your XI in a World Cup, Euro Championship, Champions League, or League season. Score points based on how far you go, goals, clean sheets, and difficulty. Results save to the leaderboard.
